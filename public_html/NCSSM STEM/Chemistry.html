Chemistry by Inquiry

This survey chemistry course includes atomic and molecular structure, chemical reactions, stoichiometry, physical properties, thermodynamics, kinetics, equilibrium, and electrochemistry. Emphasis is on developing inquiry skills required for learning science along with specific reading and writing, problem solving, and technology applications.
