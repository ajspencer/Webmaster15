Community Service

Our Work

The members of our chapter are active at NCSSM, inside of the classroom, inside our chapter and in the greater community. Members of our chapter strive to maintain excellent grades, excel in their events in TSA and impact the greater community. Members of our chapter all complete 60 hours of community service the summer of their junior or senior year. In addition to this service, the chapter engages with the community in numerous other ways. Members of our chapter volunteered in a food drive to raise money and food for a local food bank, often volunteer to help in Open Houses and often assist other students with their work. Our officers make it a priority to reinforce the importance of giving to the community.

Who We Are

Awards

Our chapter our TSA has often been recognized for our distinguished performance. We have been represented at the national conference every year within memory of our chapter's existence at NCSSM. Our members strive to do well in their events, generating great results. Because of our great environment and culture of success, winners of state events often come from our chapter.

Activities

Our chapter holds regular meetings to ensure that members are making progress on their events. We pride ourselves in being a collaborative environment; as the state competition approaches, we hold practice sessions where members can seek help from one another, enjoy pizza and focus on success. The activities of our chapter: our formal meetings, community service sessions and practice sessions are easily recognized on campus.
